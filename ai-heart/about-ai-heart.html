About AI-Heart

AI-Heart is an online platform with adventure and educational elements designed to help users understand and learn about Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology in an interactive way.
‍
Our platform encourages users to explore and apply all aspects of AI by telling the story of our main character, Xiao Shuai, and his AI "AI-Heart" as they grow, learn and explore together.Our platform features a unique AI Skill Tree system that covers a wide range of AI-related knowledge and skills. In this way, we hope that users can not only learn about the various possibilities of AI, but also experience the process of AI and human growth together.
‍
Our goal is to let more people know, understand and fall in love with AI technology. Whether you are a student, a teacher, or an ordinary person who is curious about AI, we welcome you to join the journey of Heart of AI and explore the infinite possibilities of AI together.
‍
In Heart of AI, you will not only learn about AI, but also experience the fun and challenges brought by technology, and more importantly, you will see a smarter future.
